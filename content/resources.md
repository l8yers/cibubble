Welcome to the CIbubble resources page — your launchpad for learning languages the natural way using **Comprehensible Input**.

Here you'll find my favorite tools, channels, and reading materials that make language learning feel effortless and fun.

---

## 📺 Best Free Comprehensible Input Sources

### YouTube Channels

- [Dreaming Spanish](https://www.youtube.com/@DreamingSpanish)  
  The gold standard for Spanish CI, from super beginner to advanced.

- [Ari in Beijing](https://www.youtube.com/@AriinBeijing)  
  Fantastic Mandarin CI, clearly presented with humor and context.

- [Comprehensible Russian](https://www.youtube.com/@ComprehensibleRussian)  
  Great input-based content for Russian learners at various levels.

### Podcasts

- [Chill Spanish](https://open.spotify.com/show/1iR0rxlt8iqvm0VfaO0URO)  
  Relaxed podcast made for learners using the CI method.

- [French Blabla](https://frenchblabla.org/)  
  Friendly French podcast with clear, level-appropriate speech.

---

## 🛠 Tools to Support Your CI Habit

- [Language Reactor](https://www.languagereactor.com/)  
  Adds interactive subtitles and pop-up dictionaries to YouTube/Netflix.

- [LingQ](https://www.lingq.com/)  
  Import your own CI material and read with assisted translation and word tracking.

- [Readlang](https://readlang.com/)  
  Perfect for reading articles and web content with one-click translations.
